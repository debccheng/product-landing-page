Tip Top Hats

All handcrafted in Australia

Tip Top Hats was founded in 1534 by an Australian farmer, Henry Tiptop. The original hat was crafted from kangaroo hide. The techniques of our hat crafting have since been perfected and passed down for many generations. We have even branched out into a new line of carbon-free and vegan-friendly hats to accommodate all lifestyles.
